triangle

        triangle [surface-desc]  x1 y1 z1  x2 y2 z2  x3 y3 z3
Figure 2-20

Create a (flat) triangle. The order of the vertices is important when using triangle to construct a solid object (that can be used for CSG).

In this example a "curving" form is created using flat triangles. The shape has five planes (each made up of three triangles), giving a total of fifteen triangles.

Figure 2-21

          tri-flat.ray

          sp mon 24 feb 92

        screen 300 200
        background .9 .9 .9
        light 1 point -10 10 10
